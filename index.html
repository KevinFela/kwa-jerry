Our Story

Serving since 2006

Who We Are: The Heart of the Township

Kwa Jerry Fast Food isn't just a restaurant; it's a testament to the power of a dream rooted in the community. Founded in 2006 by a man—my Father—who had a business idea, and later taken over with a vision by Jerry, our journey began with a single, clear-eyed mission: to take the soul and flavour of "kasi" food and serve it with the professionalism, hygiene, and consistency it deserves.

Starting as a small township operation, Jerry saw a gap where others saw only the status quo. He understood that while the community loved the traditional tastes of street-side meals and local favourites, they also deserved a clean, world-class environment and a business that respected their time. By combining a deep financial education with a "risk-taker" spirit, Jerry transformed those humble beginnings into a thriving Gauteng success story.

Today, we have grown to three locations across the province. While our reach has expanded, our roots remain unchanged. We still believe that food for the body is not enough—it must be food for the soul. At Kwa Jerry, we are more than a fast-food brand; we are a community partner committed to providing delicious, affordable meals served with a sincere love for our culture and our customers.

Our Core Values

By integrating the original philosophy of the founder with our modern standards, these are the pillars that support every meal we serve:

Fresh Ingredients Daily

We believe quality starts at the source. We honour the "kasi" tradition of fresh, local ingredients while maintaining high standards of hygiene and taste.

Operational Excellence

Whether it's being "always on time" or providing fast, friendly service, we respect our customers' time as much as we respect their loyalty.

A Clean & Controlled Environment

We have elevated street food by overcoming hygiene concerns, ensuring every location offers a spotless, safe, and welcoming space for the community.

Community & Soul

We serve with "sincere love." Our business is built by the township, for the township, and we strive to provide "food for the soul" in every interaction.

Financial Integrity & Growth

We use our resources constructively to ensure we remain affordable for our customers while continuing to grow our franchise empire across South Africa.
